We are very happy to announce the following keynote speaker for the International Conference on Civil Engineering Fundamentals and Applications (ICCEFA'19):

Dr. Sameh Badie

The George Washington University, USA

Dr. Sameh Badie

Dr. Sameh Badie is a professor at the Civil and Environmental Engineering Department at the George Washington University (GWU). Dr. Badie joined George Washington University in 2001. Dr. Badie has more than 30 years of experience in research and academia in the field of structural engineering.

Dr. Badie received his bachelor and master degrees in structural engineering with high honors from Ain Shams University, Cairo, Egypt. He received his Ph.D. degree in civil engineering, with a major in structural engineering, from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, in 1997.

Dr. Badie has long and successful history with research projects related to use of precast concrete deck panel systems on highway bridges. He is the principal investigator (PI) of NCHRP 12-65 and NCHRP 12-96, and a co-principal investigator (Co-PI) of NCHRP 12-41 and NCHRP 18-14, sponsored by the National Cooperative Highway Research Program of the Transportation Research Board (TRB). Many of the products developed in his research projects have been implemented on highway bridges in North America, including the NUDECK deck panel system, the hidden-pocket deck panel systems, and the large-size steel studs. Dr. Badie’s research on composite beam/slab systems lead to a major change in the AASHTO LRFD Bridge Specification, where the shear connector spacing was increased from 24 inches to 48 inches. His recent research in NCHRP 12-96 has revealed that the spacing can be extended to 72 inches, while maintaining full composite action.

Dr. Badie is the recipient of the Martin P. Korn Award, Young Educator Award, Leslie D Martin Award of Merit Award, and Certificate of Special Merit from the Precast/Prestressed Concrete Institute (PCI), the Charles Pankow Award for Innovation from the Civil Engineering Research Foundation (CERF), the ACI Structural Engineering Award from the American Concrete Institute (ACI), and the Outstanding Teacher Award from the George Washington University.

Dr. Badie is a co-author of the PCI State-of-the-Art Report on Full-depth Precast Concrete Bridge Deck Panels, and the 1st and 2nd editions of the PCI Bridge Design Manual. Dr. Badie is a co-author of many webinars sponsored by PCI and FHWA on use of precast concrete deck panel systems on highway bridges. Dr. Badie is a registered professional engineer in Maryland, Nebraska and Virginia.

Topic of Keynote: Precast Concrete Deck Panel Systems for Highway Bridges: History and New Emerging Innovations
